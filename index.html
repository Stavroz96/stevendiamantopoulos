About

My journey began early in medicine. I was fascinated with it and I started a career in emergency medicine as a EMT at my community ambulance company. I began as a student and worked my way up to an advanced life support technician. I had decided to go to medical school to become a doctor and shortly after beginning that chapter in my life the COVID epidemic began and the world collapsed into chaos. My life and dream as I knew it since a kid dissolved before my eyes and my whole world view was changed. Whilst in undergrad I had began to tinker around with coding and began some self teaching and classes. So during COVID I decided to explore that avenue some more and it turns out... I love it! Watching a project come together from scratch is sooo satisfying. Tinkering turned into passion. I took some Udemy courses and eventually joined Tech Elevator in April of 2023. I completed the course in December 2023 and came out a Full-Stack developer equipped to take on the tech industry. Today I early anticipate developing my skills even further and being able to contribute in the field of technology.
